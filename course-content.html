Introduction to Forex Trading

DEF: an online business that involves the buying and selling of global appreciating and depreciating currency pairs and commodities respectively to take advantage of the difference price fluctuations for profit generation.

ART OF PROFITING

buying before a currency, commodity appreciates selling before a currency, commodity depreciates.

UNDERSTANDING PRICE

In any given market there are two major forces responsible for price fluctuations either upwards or downwards. *Demand (scarcity) A force behind a product whose price is appreciating *Supply(excess) A force behind a product whose price is depreciating..
